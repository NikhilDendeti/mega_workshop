This workshop is ideal for young tech enthusiasts from various academic backgrounds who are eager to explore the field of data analytics.

By the end of this workshop, you'll have a fundamental understanding of data analytics, hands-on experience with key tools, and a project to enhance your resume.
We'll use popular platforms like Kaggle, Excel, Power BI, Co-Pilot for a real-world experience in data analytics and dashboard creation.
